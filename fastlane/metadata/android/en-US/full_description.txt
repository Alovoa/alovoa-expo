Native Android application for Alovoa, the open-source dating platform. 

Alovoa aims to be the first global open-source platform that enables people to easily find new friends and dates.

Our promises:
• No microtransaction
• No annoying ads
• No selling your data
• Strict filters, only see people you want to see
• Completely free (as in free speech)
• Completely free (as in free beer)
